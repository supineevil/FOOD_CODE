PASTA

Pasta is the Italian food or noodle which is made from the unleavened dough extracted from durum wheat flour combined with eggs, water and formed into various shapes. It is boiled, cooked or baked. It is also prepared with flour made from grains and cereals. The pasta is divided into fresh pasta and dried pasta.

Nutritional value :

124 g of pasta contains 196 calories, 77.04 g of moisture, 7.19 g of protein, 1.15 g of total fat, 0.33 g of ash, 38.27 g of carbohydrate, 2.2 g of total dietary fiber, 0.69 g of total sugars, 0.11 g of sucrose, 0.05 g of glucose, 0.04 g of fructose, 0.5 g of maltose and 32.25 g of starch. It has the minerals such as 9 mg of calcium, 1.59 mg of iron, 22 mg of magnesium, 72 mg of phosphorus, 55 mg of potassium, 1 mg of zinc, 0.63 mg of copper, 0.399 mg of manganese, 32.7 µg of selenium and 8.7 µg of fluoride.(more..)

PIZZA

Pizza is a type of food that was created in Italy. It is made with different toppings. Some of the most common toppings are cheese, sausages, pepperoni, vegetables, tomatoes, spices and herbs and basil. ... The piece of bread is usually called a "pizza crust".

Nutritional value :

Two slices (1/4) of a typical, 13-inch cheese and meat pizza have been shown to provide almost 1/3 of the daily recommended allowance for protein, 12-15% for vitamin A, 30-45% for thiamin, 25-30% for riboflavin, 20-30% for niacin, 40-50% for calcium, and 18-25% for iron.(more..)

LASAGNE

Lasagne, or the singular lasagna, is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of thin flat pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce) and other vegetables, cheese (which may include ricotta and parmesan), and seasonings and spices such as garlic, oregano and basil.

Nutritional value :

Restaurant, lasagna with meat, Italian contains 185 calories per 100 g serving. One serving contains 11 g of fat, 11 g of protein and 11 g of carbohydrate. The latter is 3.2 g sugar and 1.5 g of dietary fiber, the rest is complex carbohydrate. Restaurant, lasagna with meat, Italian contains 4.9 g of saturated fat and 36 mg of cholesterol per serving. 100 g of Restaurant, lasagna with meat, Italian contains 550.00 IU vitamin A, 0.7 mg of vitamin C and 0.00 mcg of vitamin D as well as 0.79 mg of iron, 197.00 mg of calcium and 255 mg of potassium.(more..)

GELATO

Gelato is a popular frozen dessert of Italian origin. It is generally made with a base of 3.25% butterfat whole milk and sugar. It is generally lower in fat than other styles of frozen desserts.
